**NOTES:**
* The prefixes of the variables names _**t**_ corresponds to time and _**f**_ corresponds to frequency.
* _**MEAN**_ Mean value.
* _**STDEV**_ Standard deviation value.
* _**N/A**_ corresponds to Not Apply.
